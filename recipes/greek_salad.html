Greek Salad

Description

Greek salad is a refreshing and vibrant dish that celebrates the flavors of the Mediterranean. It typically features ripe tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, thinly sliced red onions, and briny Kalamata olives, all tossed together in a simple dressing of extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar. The salad is elevated with the addition of creamy crumbled feta cheese and aromatic oregano, which add richness and depth of flavor. With its colorful presentation and harmonious combination of ingredients, Greek salad offers a perfect balance of textures and tastes, from the juicy tomatoes to the tangy feta cheese. Served chilled or at room temperature, it makes for a light and satisfying meal on its own or as a refreshing side dish to complement a variety of Mediterranean-inspired meals.

Ingredients

  • Ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • Cucumber, chopped
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Fresh oregano or dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine chopped tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and Kalamata olives.
  2. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar over the salad ingredients.
  3. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the salad.
  4. Season with fresh or dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients evenly.
  6. Serve the Greek salad chilled or at room temperature as a refreshing side dish or light meal.
