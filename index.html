Description

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast dish made by whisking eggs, often with a bit of milk or cream, and then cooking them in a pan over medium heat while gently stirring until they're softly set. The result is a creamy, fluffy texture with a mild flavor that can be seasoned with salt, pepper, and various optional ingredients like herbs, cheese, or vegetables for added flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
